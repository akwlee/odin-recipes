Kimchi Stew

Description

Kimchi stew is a classic Korean dish. It's also a relatively simple dish but has a lot of flavor. I like to add spam to it, which is pretty non-traditional, but super delicious. A good kimchi stew uses really fermented kimchi.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fermented kimchi
  • 1 can of spam
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • White rice of choice
  • 1 green onion (garnish)
  • 1 quarter white or yellow onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 to 3 cups of water

Steps

  1. Dice your onions, garlic, green onions, and spam.
  2. In a pot, add your sesame oil and start cooking your kimchi, garlic, and onions over medium high heat until kimchi turns translucent
  3. Add in your water
  4. Add the diced spam
  5. Cover and let simmer for 15 to 20 minutes. The longer it simmers, the more flavor develops
  6. Once done, add your thinly sliced green onions as garnish
  7. Enjoy with rice!
