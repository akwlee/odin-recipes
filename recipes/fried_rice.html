Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is an easy dish to make. It doesn't require a lot of ingredients and is very quick. Plus you can get pretty creative with it

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of day old rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 1 chicken thigh (or protein of choice)
  • 1 green onion (garnish)
  • 1 quarter white or yellow onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Optional - add veggies of choice

Steps

  1. Dice your onions, garlic, and green onions
  2. In a pan, add oil of choice and start cooking your vegetables over medium heat until onions become a bit translucent
  3. Add in your protein
  4. Once protein is cooked, add your friend rice
  5. Add in soy sauce and sesame oil
  6. Keep stirring the rice, but let it crisp up
  7. Top with a fried egg or green onions, and enjoy with hot sauces
